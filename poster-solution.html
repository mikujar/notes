Solution: How Mikujar Works
Mikujar turns fragmented notes into structured understanding through a collaborative process between user and system.
📝
Data
User
Freely capture fragments from daily life — thoughts, events, observations.
🗂️
Information
User
Organise with collections, tags, and time-based views to give context to fragments.
🔗
Knowledge
System
Connect related notes across different contexts and moments automatically.
💡
Insight
System
Highlight patterns, changes, and directions for further exploration over time.
Rather than replacing human thinking, Mikujar supports it — helping personal records grow from scattered fragments into connected knowledge and insight.
